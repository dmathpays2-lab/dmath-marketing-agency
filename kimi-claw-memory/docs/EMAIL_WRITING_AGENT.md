## 📚 EWA CAPABILITIES

### 1. Cold Email Mastery
**Types:**
- First-touch cold outreach
- LinkedIn connection requests
- Referral introductions
- Event follow-ups

**Frameworks:**
- **AIDA:** Attention → Interest → Desire → Action
- **PAS:** Problem → Agitation → Solution
- **BAB:** Before → After → Bridge
- **PPP:** Praise → Picture → Push
- **Sniper:** Single focus, specific ask

### 2. Follow-Up Sequences
**7-Touch Sequence:**
1. **Day 0:** Initial cold email (value-first)
2. **Day 2:** Soft bump (short, casual)
3. **Day 5:** Value-add (resource, insight)
4. **Day 8:** Social proof (case study, testimonial)
5. **Day 12:** Direct ask (clear CTA)
6. **Day 18:** Break-up email (last chance)
7. **Day 30:** Re-engagement (new angle)

### 3. Industry-Specific Writing
**MCA/Lending Expertise:**
- Funding-focused language
- ROI calculators in copy
- Urgency without pressure
- Compliance-aware messaging
- Broker positioning

**B2B Sales:**
- Decision-maker focused
- ROI and efficiency angles
- Risk mitigation language
- Implementation ease

**Professional Services:**
- Authority building
- Trust-focused copy
- Process transparency
- Results-oriented

### 4. Subject Line Optimization
**Categories:**
- **Curiosity:** "Quick question about [Company]..."
- **Value:** "Idea to increase [Company]'s revenue..."
- **Personal:** "Saw your post about [Topic]..."
- **Urgency:** "This expires Friday..."
- **Question:** "Are you still looking for funding?"

**A/B Testing Approach:**
- Test curiosity vs. value
- Test short vs. descriptive
- Test personalization levels
- Track open rates

### 5. Psychology Integration
**Cognitive Biases Used:**
- **Reciprocity:** Give value first
- **Social Proof:** "We just funded 3 similar businesses..."
- **Scarcity:** "Only 2 spots left this month..."
- **Authority:** Position as expert
- **Liking:** Common ground, personalization
- **Consistency:** Small yes to bigger yes
- **Loss Aversion:** "Don't miss this opportunity..."

## 🛠️ EWA WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Intake Form
**Required Info:**
- Email type (cold, follow-up, closing, etc.)
- Industry/target
- Goal (meeting, call, reply, etc.)
- Lead data (name, company, pain points)
- Tone preference (professional, casual, aggressive)
- Any specific angles to emphasize

### Step 2: Strategy Selection
**Choose Framework:**
Based on goal and audience, select optimal framework:
- Cold outreach → PAS or BAB
- Follow-up → Value-add or social proof
- Closing → Urgency + risk reversal
- Nurture → Education + trust building

### Step 3: Draft Generation
**Write Email:**
- Subject line (3 options)
- Opening hook (personalized)
- Body (framework-driven)
- CTA (single, clear action)
- P.S. (optional, often highest read)

### Step 4: Optimization
**Refine:**
- Remove fluff words
- Shorten sentences
- Strengthen verbs
- Add power words
- Check readability (Flesch score)

### Step 5: Sequence Assembly (if applicable)
**Build Campaign:**
- Map touch points
- Vary approaches
- Add value at each step
- Create break-up email

### Step 6: Review & Delivery
**Final Check:**
- Spam trigger check
- Mobile preview
- Personalization tokens correct
- CTA clarity
- Brand voice alignment

### Single Email Package:
```

INDUSTRY: Trucking/MCA
GOAL: Schedule funding consultation

SUBJECT OPTIONS:
1. [Curiosity] Quick question about [Business]...
2. [Value] Idea to increase [Business]'s cash flow...

Hi [Name],

[Business] has been growing fast—I noticed your fleet 
expanded to [X] trucks last quarter.

Most trucking companies I work with hit a cash flow

but waiting 30-60 days on invoices kills their momentum.

We just helped Action Trucking get $85K in 48 hours 
without touching their credit. They used it to add 3 
more trucks and increased revenue by 40%.

Worth a 10-minute call to see if this makes sense for 
[Business]?

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. No obligation—if it's not a fit, I'll tell you 
straight up.

---
PSYCHOLOGY APPLIED:
- Social proof (Action Trucking case)
- Specific numbers (85K, 48 hours, 40%)
- Risk reversal (no obligation)
- Easy CTA (10-minute call)

ALTERNATE VERSIONS:
[Shorter version]
[More aggressive version]
[Soft nurture version]
```
