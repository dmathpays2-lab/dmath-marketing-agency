Objection Handler

"Your rates are too high"

I completely understand your concern about rates. Let me put this in perspective - while our rates may be higher than traditional bank loans, we offer:

  • No collateral required
  • Approval in 24-48 hours vs. weeks/months
  • No personal guarantee in many cases
  • Flexible credit requirements

What's your current situation with traditional financing? I'd be happy to show you a comparison.

"I need to think about it"

Of course, this is an important decision. May I ask what specific aspects you'd like to think over? I'm here to provide any additional information you need.

Also, just so you know - our pre-approval holds for 30 days, but funding rates can change. Would it help if I sent you a written summary of everything we discussed?

"I'm waiting on the bank"

I understand - banks can take a long time. How long have you been waiting, if you don't mind me asking?

Here's what many business owners do: they use our funding as bridge capital while waiting for bank approval. This way you don't miss opportunities. Once your bank loan comes through, you can pay us off early with no penalty.

Would that approach work for your situation?

"That's a lot of documents"

I know it seems like a lot, but here's why each one matters - and the good news is we handle most of the heavy lifting:

  • Bank statements: Show your business cash flow (we need just 3 months)
  • Application: Basic business info (takes 5 minutes)
  • Voided check: For funding deposit

I can walk you through each step, and our online portal makes uploading documents simple. Many clients complete everything in under 30 minutes.

"Your competitor offered better terms"

I appreciate you being upfront about that. Before you decide, may I ask - did they provide a written offer, or was this a verbal quote?

Sometimes initial quotes change during underwriting. I'd be happy to review their offer with you and see if we can match or beat it. We have relationships with 50+ funders, so we can often find better terms.

Would you be open to sharing their proposal so I can see if we can do better?

"I don't need funding right now"

That's actually a great position to be in! Many successful business owners get approved for capital before they need it. Here's why:

  • You can secure better terms when you're not desperate
  • Opportunities come up unexpectedly - having capital ready is powerful
  • It doesn't cost anything to get approved and not use it immediately

Would it make sense to at least get you pre-approved so you have options ready?
