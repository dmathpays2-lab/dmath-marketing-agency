## 🏆 TIER 1: PET-SPECIFIC INFLUENCER PLATFORMS

### #1 - PAWJOURR (HIGHEST RECOMMENDED)
**Website:** https://pawjourr.com  
**Type:** Pet-Specific Influencer Marketplace

#### How It Works:
- 30,000+ pet creators across all species (dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, etc.)
- Brands create campaigns → creators apply → brands select
- 100% FREE for creators (no commission fees)
- $10M+ paid out to creators
- 100K+ content pieces created

#### Pricing:
- **Free tier:** Up to 10 creators/month
- **Subscription:** Simple monthly fee for brands (custom pricing available upon request)
- **Business+:** Full-service management available
- **NO commission taken from creators** (major differentiator)

#### Key Features:
- Global reach (US, UK, Singapore, Hong Kong, Malaysia)
- Bi-weekly creator payments
- End-to-end campaign management
- Content review & approval system
- Market intelligence tools

#### Pros:
✅ Largest pet-specific creator network  
✅ No creator commission = attracts top talent  
✅ Covers ALL pet types (not just dogs)  
✅ Proven track record with major brands (Hill's Science Diet, Applaws, ProBalance)  
✅ Free tier to test platform  
✅ User-friendly dashboard  

#### Cons:
❌ Less advanced breed-specific filtering than Dogfluence  
❌ Brand-side pricing not fully transparent  

#### Best For MoreMito:
**Excellent starting point** - large network, no creator fees means better engagement, and covers all pet types for mitochondria-supporting products.

## 💡 STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### For MoreMito Representatives - START HERE:

#### Phase 1: Testing (Month 1-2)
1. **Pawjourr Free Tier** - Create campaigns, work with up to 10 creators
2. **Collabstr Pro ($299)** - Test marketplace model
3. Focus on product seeding (barter) to minimize costs

#### Phase 2: Scaling (Month 3-6)
1. Upgrade Pawjourr to paid tier for unlimited access
2. Add Dogfluence if targeting specific dog breeds
3. Negotiate long-term ambassador deals with top performers

#### Phase 3: Optimization (Month 6+)
1. Consider Impact.com for affiliate + influencer tracking
2. Explore white-label solutions via Pawjourr Business+
3. Evaluate MLM partnership with PawTree for representative network

### MLM Strategy Alternative:
If MoreMito wants to build its own representative network:
- Study PawTree's compensation structure
- Position products as premium supplements
- Focus on science/education-based selling
- Target pet professionals (trainers, groomers, vets)
