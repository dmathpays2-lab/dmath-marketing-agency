## 🔥 VERTICAL 1: MCA (Merchant Cash Advance)

### Daily Automation Tasks

**Morning (8:00 AM):**
```
1. Scrape business listings:
   - Yelp (restaurants, contractors, service businesses)
   - Google Maps (high-revenue businesses in your area)
   - Industry directories
   
2. Qualify prospects:
   - Filter: 2+ years in business, $15K+ monthly revenue
   - Check: Recent growth indicators, expansion signs
   - Score: A (hot), B (warm), C (nurture)
   
3. Enrich data:
   - Find owner name and contact info
   - Check website for funding needs signals
   - Look for credit challenges or growth opportunities
```

**Afternoon (2:00 PM):**
```
4. Send personalized outreach:
   - Email #1: "Quick funding option for [Business Name]"
   - LinkedIn connection to owner
   - SMS (if available): Short value prop
   
5. Track responses:
   - Auto-log in CRM
   - Schedule follow-up based on engagement
   - Alert you for hot leads (A-score responses)
```

**Evening (6:00 PM):**
```
6. Daily report:
   - New prospects found: [X]
   - Outreach sent: [Y]
   - Responses received: [Z]
   - Hot leads requiring your attention: [List]
```

### Lead Sources
- Yelp (restaurants, retail, services)
- Google Maps (local businesses)
- Chamber of Commerce directories
- Industry-specific sites (HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack)
- LinkedIn (business owners)
- Facebook Business pages

### Qualification Criteria
✅ 2+ years in business  
✅ $15,000+ monthly revenue  
✅ Visible growth/expansion  
✅ Seasonal business (needs working capital)  
✅ Recent equipment purchases (cash flow needs)
