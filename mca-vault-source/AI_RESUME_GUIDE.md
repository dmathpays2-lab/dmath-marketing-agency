# 🤖 AI RESUME & JOB SEARCH GUIDE
## Kimi's Master Reference for Career Tasks

---

## [Why This Matters]
90% of companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes. If a resume isn't optimized with the right keywords, it gets rejected by a robot. Use the following prompts to beat the ATS and prepare for interviews.

---

## [Prompt 1: Get Your Match Score]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Act as a senior recruiter with 10+ years of experience. Analyze my resume against this job description. Give me a match score out of 100 and list the top 5 missing keywords that I need to include to pass ATS filters.
```

---

## [Prompt 2: Optimize Your Experience Section]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Rewrite my experience section to naturally include those keywords. Use the Google XYZ formula for every bullet point: "Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y] by doing [Z]." Focus on quantifiable results.
```

**Example:**
"Increased regional sales by 34% (from $2M to $2.68M) by leading a cross-functional team of 8 and implementing a new CRM system."

---

## [Prompt 3: Beat the ATS]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Act as an applicant tracking system filter. Scan my resume. Tell me which sections a bot would struggle to read, which formatting (tables, text boxes, headers, footers, images, unusual fonts) might cause issues, and what I need to fix for 100% compatibility.
```

---

## [Prompt 4: Technical Interview Prep]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Act as the hiring manager for this role. Ask me the 3 hardest technical questions you would ask. Then give me the perfect response based on my background and the job description.
```

---

## [Prompt 5: Killer Cover Letter]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Write a cover letter under 300 words that: opens with a strong hook, highlights my top 3 achievements, explains why I'm uniquely qualified, and ends with a call to action.
```

---

## [Prompt 6: LinkedIn Headline]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Write 5 different LinkedIn headlines under 120 characters that include relevant industry keywords and show my unique value proposition.
```

---

## [Prompt 7: LinkedIn "About" Section]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Write a conversational "About" section under 200 words that tells my professional story, highlights achievements, and includes industry keywords.
```

---

## [Prompt 8: Behavioral Questions]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Act as an interviewer. Give me the top 5 behavioral questions likely to be asked. Provide a strong answer for each using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) based on my resume.
```

---

## [Prompt 9: Salary Negotiation]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Help me determine if a salary offer is fair based on industry standards, write a professional counter-offer email, and suggest a realistic salary range.
```

---

## [Prompt 10: Follow Up Email]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Write a professional follow-up email (under 150 words) to send 24-48 hours after an interview.
```

---

## [Prompt 11: Identify Companies]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Suggest 20 companies (startups to large corps) in my field and location likely to be hiring based on my skills.
```

---

## [Prompt 12: Company Research]
**Use this prompt:**
```
Research a specific company: summarize what they do, recent news/achievements, culture, 3 smart questions to ask, and potential challenges I can address.
```

---

## 📝 KEY FORMULAS TO REMEMBER

### Google XYZ Formula:
"Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y] by doing [Z]"

### STAR Method:
- **S**ituation
- **T**ask
- **A**ction
- **R**esult

### ATS Best Practices:
- No tables, text boxes, headers, footers
- No images or unusual fonts
- Use standard section headings
- Include exact keywords from job description
