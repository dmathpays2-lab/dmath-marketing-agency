# Disability-Friendly Tech Sales Jobs Report
## Best Tech Companies for Sales Roles with Assistive Technology

**Date:** March 17, 2026  
**Research Focus:** Disability accommodations and accessibility for tech sales positions  
**Target Profile:** Job seekers with typing limitations using Dragon/voice dictation tools

---

## Executive Summary

This report evaluates five leading tech companies for their disability inclusion practices, assistive technology support, and suitability for sales professionals who rely on voice dictation tools like Dragon NaturallySpeaking, Dragon Anywhere, or AI-powered alternatives.

### Key Findings:
- **Salesforce** leads in established disability inclusion infrastructure (DEI 100 score)
- **HubSpot** offers the most voice-friendly CRM with built-in dictation
- **Zoom** provides excellent phone-heavy sales roles (less typing required)
- **OpenAI** and **Anthropic** are newer with less established policies but show commitment

## Disclosure Strategy & Legal Rights

### When to Disclose Your Disability

**OPTION 1: During Application (Recommended for These Companies)**
- ✅ All five companies have explicit accommodation statements
- ✅ Shows proactive communication style
- ✅ Allows for interview accommodations if needed
- ✅ Legal protection under ADA applies immediately

**OPTION 2: After Job Offer**
- ✅ Can demonstrate value first
- ✅ Legal right to accommodations still protected
- ⚠️ May delay getting needed tools set up

**OPTION 3: After Starting (Not Recommended)**
- ❌ Can create unnecessary stress
- ❌ May delay accommodation implementation

### Recommended Approach for Tech Sales:

**Application Stage:**
- Mention only if you need interview accommodations
- Use standard application process

**After Offer/Before Start:**
- Disclose disability and accommodation needs
- Request assistive technology setup
- Ask for CRM training with voice tools

### How to Request Accommodations Professionally

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Request for Reasonable Accommodations - [Your Name]

Dear [HR/Manager Name],

I'm excited to accept the [Position] offer and am looking forward to contributing to the team.

I'm writing to request reasonable accommodations under the ADA. I have a disability that affects my ability to type, and I use voice dictation software (Dragon NaturallySpeaking) to perform computer-based tasks efficiently.

The accommodations I need are:
1. Permission to use my personal assistive technology (Dragon NaturallySpeaking)
2. Installation of voice dictation software on work devices
3. Flexible documentation methods for CRM updates (voice notes acceptable)
4. [Any other specific needs]

These accommodations will enable me to perform all essential job functions effectively. I'm happy to discuss this further and work with IT to ensure smooth implementation.

Thank you for your support.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Legal Rights Under ADA for Remote Workers

**You Have the Right To:**
- ✅ Reasonable accommodations for your disability
- ✅ Use assistive technology to perform job functions
- ✅ Work from home if it's a reasonable accommodation
- ✅ Not be discriminated against due to disability
- ✅ Confidentiality - employer cannot share your disability status

**Reasonable Accommodations Include:**
- Assistive technology (Dragon, screen readers, etc.)
- Modified work schedules
- Voice-to-text software
- Alternative communication methods
- Ergonomic equipment

**Process:**
1. Request accommodation (can be informal)
2. Engage in "interactive process" with employer
3. Provide medical documentation if requested
4. Employer must provide accommodation unless it causes "undue hardship"

**Resources:**
- EEOC: www.eeoc.gov/disability-discrimination
- Job Accommodation Network (JAN): askjan.org
- ADA National Network: adata.org
